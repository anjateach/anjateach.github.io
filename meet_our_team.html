About Anja

I've been a passionate educator since I taught my first swimming lesson at 16 years old. Since then, I've worked as a lead camp counsellor for Children's Village of Ottawa-Carleton, a teaching assistant for Carleton University's School of Mathematics and Statistics, and a summer school teacher for college math at Adult High School. Most recently I was a resource teacher for math studies and HL and SL biology, and an IB teacher for HL and SL math at Stratford Hall, an IB World school in Vancouver, BC. Simply put, it is my life's work to work in tandem with anyone with the desire to learn to help achieve their goals. Along with education passion and experience, I worked as a Research Assistant at Carleton University in the field of crytography.

About Steven

Steven has been a self-employed tutor since finishing high school. He also loves helping others; working as a Co-op Peer Helper at Carleton University. Starting in his 3rd year of university he began working as a teaching assistant for 11 total courses in statistics, calculus, and linear algebra during his time at Carleton University's School of Mathematics and Statistics. Steven recently completed his MSc Statistics at Simon Fraser University. Steven now spends his daytime applying his statistics and computer science at Ottawa's top tech employer, Shopify.
